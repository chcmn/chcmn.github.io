Pixer

Pixer is a dating app where users need to talk / chat with each other to step by step unlock pictures / parts of a picture of the person they are chatting with. Instead of "matching" based on pictures, you get the chance to first talk to the person before the pictures matter. In this way, you need to "match" those you are interested in based on their interests and what they write.
